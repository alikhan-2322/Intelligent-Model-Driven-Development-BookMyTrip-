Based on the provided use-cases and data model for the BookMyTrip application, here is a list of possible REST API endpoints:

1. **Trips**

   - **GET /trips**
     - Retrieve a list of all available trips.

   - **GET /trips/{id}**
     - Retrieve details of a specific trip by its unique identifier.

   - **GET /trips/search**
     - Search for trips based on destination, date range, or other criteria.

2. **Comparing Prices**

   - **GET /trips/comparePrices**
     - Compare prices of trips based on specific criteria like destination or date range.

3. **Bookings**

   - **POST /bookings**
     - Make a booking for a trip.

   - **GET /bookings**
     - Retrieve a list of all bookings made by the user.

   - **GET /bookings/{id}**
     - Retrieve details of a specific booking by its unique identifier.

   - **DELETE /bookings/{id}**
     - Cancel an existing booking by its unique identifier.

4. **Users**

   - **GET /users/{id}**
     - Retrieve details of a specific user by their unique identifier.

   - **POST /users**
     - Register a new user.

   - **PUT /users/{id}**
     - Update details of an existing user.

5. **Payments**

   - **POST /payments**
     - Process a payment for a booking.

   - **GET /payments/{id}**
     - Retrieve details of a specific payment by its unique identifier.

6. **Reviews**

   - **POST /reviews**
     - Submit a review for a trip.

   - **GET /reviews/trip/{tripId}**
     - Retrieve all reviews for a specific trip by its unique identifier.

   - **GET /reviews/user/{userId}**
     - Retrieve all reviews submitted by a specific user.

These endpoints encompass functionality for searching trips, booking trips, and managing user and payment information, as well as submitting and retrieving reviews.
